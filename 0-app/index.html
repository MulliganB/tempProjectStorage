About Us

Welcome to RWH Motorcycles of Lincoln Ltd, Lincoln’s premier motorcycle specialist garage. If you’re in the Lincoln area and your motorcycle is in need of repairs or servicing then please don’t hesitate to give us a call. You can bring your motorbike or scooter straight to us or, for those who require it we offer a collection and delivery service for your convenience.

We pride ourselves on our reliability and the quality of our workmanship. We will always endeavor to get work done promptly so you can get back on the road without delay. We perform repairs and servicing, MOTs, wheel building and more.

We’re the only motorbike garage in Lincolnshire to have the honor of membership of the Bridgestone Bikers Club, so your bike’s in the hands of true enthusiasts.

Services

Wheel Building

We stock leading brands, including Bridgestone, Pirelli, Avon and Continental and have the skills to build you a wheel if you need something in particular.

MOT

If your bike is due for its annual MOT and you have any concerns, our team of qualified mechanics will give you advice and guidance to make sure that it passes.

Accident Repairs

If you’ve recently been unfortunate enough to have been involved in an accident, you’ll want to get your motorbike back on the road as soon as possible.
